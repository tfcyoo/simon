This test measures the ability to integrate information.

You'll be presented three-digit numbers in all 9 cells of a 3x3 grid. One of these numbers will be replaced by a new one every 2 seconds. Your task is to press the space bar whenever three numbers in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) end on the same last digit (e.g., 234, 894, and 434 in the same column).

Please respond as accurately and quickly as possible.
