Instruction

This test measures the ability to remember associations between patterns and locations.

Your task is to memorise the positions of 2 to 4 sequentially presented patterns.
Afterwards, you will be asked to indicate whether probes shown match the patterns shown at that location before. Please respond using the arrow keys on the keyboard:

Press the backward arrow key if the probe's pattern matches the pattern previously shown at that position.

Press the forward arrow key if the probe's pattern does not match the pattern previously shown at that position.

Please press the forward arrow key to go to the next page.
