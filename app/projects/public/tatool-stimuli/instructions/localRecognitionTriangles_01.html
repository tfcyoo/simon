Instruction

This test measures the ability to remember associations between colours and locations.

Your task is to memorise the positions of 2 to 5 sequentially presented coloured triangles in a grid.
Afterwards, you will be asked to indicate whether the colours of a series of probes match the colours of the triangles shown before. Please respond using the arrow keys on the keyboard:

Press the backward arrow key if the probe's colour matches that of the triangle previously shown at that position.

Press the forward arrow key if the probe's colour does not match that of the triangle previously shown at that position.

Please press the forward arrow key to go to the next page.
